<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>
  Adds instant Steam store previews when you hover over supported game titles on CS.RIN.RU.
  Browse Steam media, ratings, tags, store links, and quick actions without leaving the page.
</p>

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
    <li>
        <strong>Instant Hover Preview</strong><br>
        Displays Steam media, date released, and short game description when you hover over a supported game title.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Steam Media Carousel</strong><br>
        Starts with the official Steam header image, then lets you browse screenshots and click-to-play videos when available.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Theatre Mode</strong><br>
        Opens screenshots and trailers in a larger Steam-style viewer with navigation, video controls, fullscreen support, and footer actions.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>See Steam Ratings</strong><br>
        Instantly view the game's review score (like ★★★★☆ "Mostly Positive") and the total number of reviews right in the preview.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Popular Steam Tags</strong><br>
        Fetches up to 5 popular tags from Steam (like "RPG", "Open World") and shows them below the Steam Ratings.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Quick Actions</strong><br>
        Includes direct "🎮 Open on Steam" and "↗️ Open Latest Page" actions in both the hover card and theatre footer.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>AppID & SteamDB Utility</strong><br>
        Shows the matched Steam AppID, lets you click it to copy, and adds a compact SteamDB shortcut beside it.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Colored CS.RIN.RU Thread Tags</strong><br>
        Shows bracketed topic tags in the hover card using <a href="https://github.com/SubZeroPL/cs-rin-ru-enhanced-mod" rel="nofollow noopener">CS.RIN.RU Enhanced</a>-style colors.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Forum-Aware Title Cleanup</strong><br>
        Cleans topic labels like "[Info]", "[CRACKED]", "[NOT CRACKED]", build/update text, and platform notes before searching Steam.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Smart Steam Matching</strong><br>
        Prefers the main Steam game page over DLC, upgrade pages, season passes, and store extras.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Snappy & Lightweight</strong><br>
        Previews are cached and preloaded for smooth browsing without constantly bothering Steam, including media metadata when available.
    </li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Usage</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Navigate to a supported CS.RIN.RU forum page.</li>
  <li>Hover over a supported game title.</li>
  <li>Browse Steam media, open theatre mode, or use the included actions directly.</li>
</ol>
