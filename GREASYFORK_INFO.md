<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>
  Adds instant Steam store previews when you hover over game topic titles on CS.RIN.RU.
</p>

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
    <li>
        <strong>Instant Hover Preview</strong><br>
        Displays Steam media, date released, and short game description when you hover over a topic title.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Steam Media Carousel</strong><br>
        Starts with the official Steam header image, then lets you browse screenshots and click-to-play videos when available.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>See Steam Ratings</strong><br>
        Instantly view the game's review score (like ★★★★☆ "Mostly Positive") and the total number of reviews right in the preview.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Popular Steam Tags</strong><br>
        Fetches up to 5 popular tags from Steam (like "RPG", "Open World") and shows them below the Steam Ratings.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>CS.RIN.RU Topic Actions</strong><br>
        Includes a direct “🎮 Open on Steam” link and a “↗️ Open Latest Page” link that jumps to the latest page of the CS.RIN.RU thread.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>AppID & SteamDB Utility</strong><br>
        Shows the matched Steam AppID, lets you click it to copy, and adds a compact SteamDB shortcut beside it.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Colored CS.RIN.RU Thread Tags</strong><br>
        Shows bracketed topic tags in the hover card using OG CS.RIN.RU Enhanced-style colors.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Forum-Aware Title Cleanup</strong><br>
        Cleans topic labels like "[Info]", "[CRACKED]", "[NOT CRACKED]", build/update text, and platform notes before searching Steam.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Base Game Matching</strong><br>
        Prefers the main Steam game page over DLC pages, upgrade pages, season passes, and store extras.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Snappy & Lightweight</strong><br>
        Previews are cached and preloaded for smooth browsing without constantly bothering Steam, including media metadata when available.
    </li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Usage</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Navigate to a supported CS.RIN.RU forum page.</li>
  <li>Hover over a game topic title in the Topics section.</li>
  <li>Use the instant Steam preview, or open the Steam page / latest topic page directly.</li>
</ol>
